Our Story

Adventist Academy Solutions was founded by a career school business manager who has lived inside the operational reality of small Adventist academies.

We have closed month-end books under pressure, managed student accounts and collections, worked through audits, balanced cash flow during tight months, and sat in board meetings answering hard financial questions with incomplete data.

We understand the tension school leaders face every day: limited budgets, increasing compliance requirements, understaffed business offices, and the constant pressure to ‚Äújust make it work‚Äù without compromising the mission.

This organization exists to change that dynamic.

Rather than treating accounting as a clerical task, we approach it as strategic infrastructure‚Äîthe system that enables leadership to make informed decisions, surface hidden risks, and steward limited resources intentionally.

Why Exclusively Adventist Schools?

We serve Adventist K‚Äì12 schools exclusively because this environment is fundamentally different from public education or generic private schools. Effective financial management here requires denominational fluency, operational context, and cultural understanding that general accounting firms simply do not have.

  • Mission-driven operations where every financial decision impacts ministry

To equip Adventist academies with professional financial management that enables strategic decision-making, strengthens stewardship, and frees leadership to focus on educational mission rather than financial firefighting.

Our Team Approach

Adventist Academy Solutions operates as a professional team model designed specifically for continuity, reliability, and risk reduction in small school environments. Every team member:

  • Shares Adventist values and understands denominational culture
  • Specializes in education accounting with systems expertise (AASI.NET, FACTS, APS)
  • Maintains professional standards with continuing education and quality controls
  • Commits to confidentiality and professional ethics
  • Operates with insurance protection ($2M professional liability + cyber coverage)
